Secure and Scalable

Open Biometric Broker Introduction

This OBE hub leverages best of breed open source standards to allow the interchange of any biometric information between organisation. It is designed for ease of implementation and connectivity with existing legacy systems.

This facilitates the secure exchange and matching of biometric data between organisations. Connecting organisations may have any variety of different supporting biometric systems types and the hub will exchange formats to suit the receiving biometric system.

The hub is designed to operate with any biometric, and has direct support currently for fingerprint and facial data. Scalability, privacy and security considerations are explicitly built into the solution.

Advantages

  • support for open biometric standards; and
  • control over access and audit; and
  • secure transactions; and
  • no hub storage of transmitted biometrics; and
  • support both authentication and identification models; and
  • the ability to scale infrastructure

There are several technical challenges in promoting the interoperability of different biometric systems and ensuring compatibility with existing legacy systems and future developments:

  • format of the transmitted biometric data (template information) and any associated biographic meta data;
  • mechanisms and protocols used to transmit the data and undertake matching;
  • control and security of data in transit;
  • connection with existing and future biometric algorithms;
  • mismatching of biographics where authentication is being undertaken (i.e. surnames spelt slightly differently in different agencies);
  • protection of legally assumed identities; and
  • different quality standards in different biometric databases.
